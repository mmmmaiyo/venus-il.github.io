Want to be a mentor?

Join us in helping shape the future of technology by sharing your knowledge and helping student entrepreneurs grow.

The Process

1. First application

Fill out our form and let us know more about you. It includes helpful templates to make filling in information easy and stress-free.

2. Initial interview

Looking good on paper isn't everything. We'll invite you to a video call with our core team members and see how well you align with our values.

3. Assessment

Next we'll give you a few scenarios to chew through that will let us know how you work and the kind of help you'll be able to provide.

4. Final interview

You're almost done. At this stage of the process, you'll get to have a short cal with our founder and core team to establish a healthy relationship and make sure you're all set up and ready to start mentoring.
